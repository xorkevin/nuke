# Tooltip

`Tooltip` is used to display auxiliary related information on hover.

<Preview>
  <Story name="tooltip">
    <Tooltip tooltip="some hover text">Hover over me</Tooltip>
  </Story>
</Preview>

`Tooltip`s may be shown on any of the four sides of an element using the
`position` prop.

<Preview>
  <Story name="tooltip position">
    <div>
      <div>
        <Tooltip position="top" tooltip="some hover text">Hover over me</Tooltip>
      </div>
      <div>
        <Tooltip position="left" tooltip="some hover text">Hover over me</Tooltip>
      </div>
      <div>
        <Tooltip position="right" tooltip="some hover text">Hover over me</Tooltip>
      </div>
      <div>
        <Tooltip position="bottom" tooltip="some hover text">Hover over me</Tooltip>
      </div>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>
